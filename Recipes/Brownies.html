Brownies

Description

A brownie recipe I made after finding every one I tried unsatisfactory. The secret ingredient is milo powder.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milo powder
  • Coffee Powder
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Cocoa

Steps

  1. Melt the butter and stir in choc chips until melted
  2. Combine butter mixture with other wet ingredient Do not overmix
  3. Mix in dry ingredients
  4. Add to lined pan and bake at 180C for 20 minutes
